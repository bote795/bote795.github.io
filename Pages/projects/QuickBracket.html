QuickBracket

Tired of having unmobile friendly brackets for tournaments? Well thats why QuickBracket was made! Its an easy way to create breackets and have people join them.

QuickBracket is a project that Jeremy, Devin, and myself worked on at the Texas A&M HackerBowl. It is a nice way to create brackets and have people view it on there mobile and join. There is other functionalities that will be added later and actually have the website up.
